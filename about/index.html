A look behind AnimeHost

1. Why i developed AnimeHost?

Hi, my name is Felipe Ribeiro, i'm 15 years old and the creator of AnimeHost. I always loved animes since i watched Death Note, and i prefer animes over series and movies, because of many varieties of stories (Even though now there are only isekais). So when the "Bitcoin Agiota" Evandro said that we need to create a website for the TCM i choose an Anime Streming Website, since it's to do something boring, at least that be something i like. So, what problems the AnimeHost tries to solve?

2. Design

All the Website design was thought to be as minialist as possible, but still enjoyable. The wireframe of the site was made in Adobe XD (great program) and the design was always made following a design pattern. The site can adapt to Smartphones and PCs.

3. No "5min games" ADs

Unlike other anime sites animehost has no ads, and no subscription, all to bring you the best possible service.
